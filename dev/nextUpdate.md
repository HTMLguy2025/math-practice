# Long-Term Goal
Students should be able to use this website for practicing various math operations,
not necessarily learning the concepts in the first place.

---

# Exercises by Level

## Level 1 (Grade 1)

### Counting & Number Sense
- Counting forward and backward to 120
- Counting on from any given number
- Ordering numbers on a number line
- Odd and even numbers (basic introduction)

### Place Value
- Tens and ones
- Adding and subtracting multiples of 10

### Addition & Subtraction
- Addition within 20
- Subtraction within 20

### Comparison
- Comparing two-digit numbers (greater than, less than, equal to)

### Measurement & Time
- Telling time to the hour and half hour

---

## Level 2 (Grade 2)

### Counting & Number Sense
- Skip counting (2s, 5s, 10s, 100s)
- Odd and even numbers
- Comparing three-digit numbers

### Place Value
- Hundreds, tens, ones

### Early Multiplication
- Arrays (conceptual foundation for multiplication)

### Measurement & Time
- Telling time to the nearest 5 minutes
- Counting money (coins and bills)

### Data
- Basic bar graphs and picture graphs

---

## Level 3 (Grade 3)

### Fractions
- Halves, thirds, fourths, sixths, eighths
- Fractions on a number line

### Rounding
- To the nearest 10 and 100

### Measurement & Time
- Elapsed time (how much time has passed)
- Measuring mass and liquid volume
- Area and perimeter of simple shapes

### Data
- Scaled bar graphs and picture graphs

---

## Level 4 (Grade 4)

### Fractions
- Equivalent fractions
- Comparing fractions with different denominators
- Adding and subtracting fractions with like denominators
- Mixed numbers and improper fractions

### Decimals
- Tenths and hundredths
- Comparing decimals

### Number Theory
- Factors and multiples
- Prime and composite numbers

### Geometry
- Angle measurement (degrees)
- Lines, rays, line segments, parallel and perpendicular lines
- Symmetry
- Area and perimeter of rectangles

---

## Level 5 (Grade 5)

### Fractions
- Adding and subtracting with unlike denominators
- Multiplying fractions and mixed numbers
- Dividing fractions (unit fractions ÷ whole numbers and vice versa)

### Decimals
- All four operations (add, subtract, multiply, divide)
- Powers of 10 (place value relationships)

### Order of Operations
- PEMDAS (no exponents)

### Number Sense
- Introduction to negative numbers on a number line
- Numerical patterns and rules

### Geometry & Graphing
- Coordinate planes (plotting and reading points)
- Volume of rectangular prisms
- Classifying 2D shapes (triangles, quadrilaterals)

### Measurement
- Converting units (customary and metric)

---

## Simple Operations
- Addition (1, 2, 3-digit)
- Subtraction (1, 2, 3-digit)
- Multiplication (1, 2, 3-digit)
- Division (2, 3-digit)
